DAY-ONE SOC CONTRIBUTION

What I can do on day one

Entry-level, lab-evidenced capabilities I can bring into junior SOC or security analyst work without overstating production experience.

01

Alert triage

Review alerts, identify affected users and hosts, separate clear signal from noise, and record the decision path.

02

Timeline reconstruction

Build event sequences from Windows, Sysmon-style, network, Zeek, and Wireshark evidence to explain what happened.

03

SPL / KQL querying

Write and adapt basic detection queries for failed logons, suspicious PowerShell, outbound traffic, and host activity review.

04

IOC enrichment

Check IPs, domains, URLs, and hashes against reputation sources while documenting confidence and evidence limits.

05

Escalation notes

Produce concise analyst notes covering summary, evidence, scope, severity, uncertainty, and recommended next action.

06

False-positive review

Compare alert behaviour against baseline, business context, expected administration, and change activity before escalation.
